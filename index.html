About Me

Hi, my name is Ivan Ansari, i was born in Madrid, raised in Minnesota and ended up coming back to Spain (my mother really missed the weather back here). I have quite an inquisitive mind and i really love learning new things from all kinds of fields! I ended up studying biology and due to my passion in tech in general but more specifically computers, i ended up getting a master's in bioinformatics which i saw as an interesting mix and a way to get introduced into coding and using this skillset to mostly study genomes and try to obtain new information from huge chunks of data.

Through the next few years i've been mostly working in the field of bioinformatics in a reasearch team from the University of Valencia and the Foundation for the Promotion of Health and Biomedical Research of the Valencian Region (FISABIO). During this lapse of time i've been using mostly Python, Bash and R mixed with a few other bioinformatic tools to offer bioinformatic support to the group and later on as a project, develop a web application using R Shiny to datamine public health records and scan for possible outbreaks occurring for a certain microorganism and geographical area applying statistical methodologies, obtaining a possible application of automated surveillance of outbreaks and a tool for researchers.

During my time as a bioinformatician, my passion for coding grew and i decided to try and refocus my career towards web development, starting out a brand new adventure learning through online courses (among those courses i've completed Colt Steele's "The Web Developer Bootcamp 2021" based on the MERN stack) and documentation while building up my own projects.

Formula Now

Formula Now is a project i built due to my passion for Formula One. It offers up to date data regarding the sport's standings, calendar and wikipedia information for both drivers and teams. It also allows to explore results from past seasons.

During the construction of the site i got to practice a bit more the technologies i've been learning, mostly React, and trying out for the first time Bootstrap on React. Also in a attempt to reduce requests towards Ergast's API, i decided to use session storage to store information retrieved and avoid further requests if possible.
